Scan-first layout

Same data as the form version, but ordered the way a receiver actually works a truck — and broken into quick steps instead of one long scroll.

  • Step 1 Scan — big scan target first; capture every tracking # in seconds.
  • Step 2 Parties — shipper (searches shippers, try "Nike") and consignee (searches customers). Receipt # appears the moment a consignee is picked.
  • Step 3 Cargo — big steppers and dropdowns, almost no typing.
  • Step 4 Condition & items — Short / Damaged, plus the Inventory tool: capture each item and it auto-fills the description.
  • Step 5 Review & sign — summary, WH LOC, Notes (auto-filled from captured items), signature if needed, then submit. The print panel stays open so you can print labels (1 per piece, editable) as many times as you want before starting a New receipt.

Why this is more ergonomic

  • Online/sync status lives in the top bar (built for offline-first queuing).
  • Big touch targets for gloves; steppers/toggles over the keyboard.
  • Next stays disabled until the step's required field is done, so a half receipt can't be submitted.
